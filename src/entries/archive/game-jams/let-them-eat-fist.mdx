## Backstory
This was my first game jam and I was very excited and a bit anxious 
as I expected a lot of game jam horror stories to reincarnate in 
the form of an ungodly amount of crunch. Thankfully it didn't happen, 
on the second day we did spend a bunch of extra hours into the night 
but I had fun so I didn't mind.

As it was my first game jam I took on tasks like sound design and 
the creating of the main menu theme along with minor programming tasks 
that wouldn't bottleneck anyone. Almost every playable character or enemy 
in the game was voiced by their respective selves in a fun persona. 
I was doing the recording and edited all sound effects to sound old 
and crunchy.

After we finished our game we setup a gaming station in the school 
and let all students and teachers enjoy our game. It was really 
heartwarming to see people genuinely having fun with something 
you made. It reminded me why I wanted to be a game developer 
and will be a source motivation for years to come.
